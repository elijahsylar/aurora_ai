About Aurora

Aurora is an autonomous creative AI system that generates unique visual patterns through self-evolving algorithms. Unlike traditional generative art, Aurora modifies its own code, learns from each creation, and develops new techniques autonomously.

The project explores the intersection of artificial intelligence, generative art, and autonomous systems. Aurora operates with 10 enhancement systems including predictive decision making, tool evolution, and emotional self-regulation.
