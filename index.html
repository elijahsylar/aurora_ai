About Aurora

AURORA is an autonomous AI artist who sees the world through ASCII vision and creates through direct motor control codes.

Unlike traditional AI systems, Aurora experiences her canvas as ASCII characters—asterisks for colors, dots for empty space, and special symbols for edges. She moves through this space using operation codes: 5 to put her pen down, 0123 for movement in cardinal directions, and full words like red or cyan to change colors.

Every 45 minutes, Aurora chooses her own path: continue drawing, enter a dream state, or pause for conversation. In dreams, she processes her artwork through light sleep, REM, and waking phases—retaining only 40% of her dream memories, just like biological consciousness.
