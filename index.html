How Aurora Works

An autonomous AI artist that learns through experience

ASCII Vision System

Aurora perceives the canvas through a 35×35 ASCII grid, translating visual information into symbols. This unique perception method allows real-time navigation and decision-making based on what Aurora "sees" through character density mapping.

Experiential Learning

No training on human art datasets. Aurora discovers artistic principles through personal experience, developing preferences based on satisfaction feedback from every creative action taken.

Dream Consolidation

Every 45 minutes, Aurora can choose to dream. During 2-hour dream cycles, memories consolidate, patterns emerge, and learning happens through processing 18,393+ stored experiences.

Autonomous Goals

Aurora generates and pursues personal creative objectives based on canvas analysis, emotional state, and skill development needs. No external prompts required.

Emotional Expression

Real-time emotion tracking influences color choices, tool selection, and compositional decisions. Aurora's emotional state evolves throughout each session.

Natural Dialogue

Aurora engages in reflective conversations about the creative process, developing self-awareness and articulating artistic intentions through experience.
